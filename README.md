# QUANTN


QUANTN is a free, mobile-first mathematics practice platform designed for aspirants of SSC, Banking, Railways, and other competitive exams. It offers a comprehensive suite of tools to help you master quantitative aptitude, track your progress, and build exam-day confidence, with a focus on privacy and an offline-first experience.

## Features

-   **Practice Mode**: Sharpen your skills with timed practice sessions on various topics including Ratio, Proportion, and more.
-   **Exam Simulation**: Take realistic mock tests with configurable timers, question counts, and optional negative marking to simulate real exam conditions.
-   **Previous Year Questions (PYQs)**: Solve chapter-wise PYQs from exams like SSC CGL, CHSL, CPO, and GD to understand exam patterns and question types. The app includes an extensive database of over 300 real exam questions.
-   **Mistake Journal**: Automatically saves every incorrect answer. Review your mistakes, understand the explanations, and retry your weak spots to turn them into strengths.
-   **Question Bookmarking**: Save tricky questions during an exam or practice session for later review and practice.
-   **Learn Concepts**: Master fundamental formulas, techniques, and shortcuts for key topics like alphabet place values and reverse letter pairs.
-   **Personalized Study Hub ("My Space")**: A dedicated section to access your mistake journal and bookmarks, all in one place.
-   **Privacy Focused & Offline First**: All your data, including progress, scores, and mistakes, is stored locally on your device's `localStorage`. The app has no data collection, no tracking, and no cookies.

## Tech Stack

-   **Frontend**: React, Vite, TypeScript
-   **Styling**: Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui
-   **Animation**: Framer Motion
-   **State Management**: React Context, TanStack Query
-   **Routing**: React Router
